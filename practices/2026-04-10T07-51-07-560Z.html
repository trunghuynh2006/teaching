A canvas app is the correct choice because it supports device integration such as the camera control for capturing images and provides full flexibility to build forms that support create, read, update, and delete (CRUD) operations on data stored in Microsoft Dataverse or other connected sources. A model-driven app is optimized for data-driven scenarios but does not provide direct integration with device hardware such as cameras. Power Automate desktop flows are used for robotic process automation (RPA), not for building interactive apps. Power BI apps are designed for analytics and reporting, not for transactional CRUD or image capture.
