You have a Power Platform environment that uses Microsoft Dataverse.

You need to ensure that users cannot access Dataverse data from outside of your company’s Microsoft Entra tenant.

What should you configure?

Select only one answer.

The correct answer is to configure a Microsoft Entra Conditional Access policy, which allows you to enforce tenant-based access requirements such as blocking access from unmanaged devices, external networks, or accounts outside your company’s tenant. Security groups manage permissions but cannot enforce sign-in restrictions. Password protection policies help prevent weak or compromised passwords but do not govern access to environment data. DLP policies restrict connector usage in Power Platform apps but cannot block tenant-level data access.
