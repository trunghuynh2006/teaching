You have a Power Platform environment.

You need to assign users a role that allows them to manage the environment and its data. The solution must follow the principle of least privilege.

Which role should you assign?

Select only one answer.

The Environment Admin role provides permissions to manage all environment settings, security, and Dataverse data while following the principle of least privilege. System Administrator grants full access to all environments and is broader than required. Environment Maker only provides the ability to create apps, flows, and other resources but not manage the environment or its data. Delegate is a limited role used for scenarios such as enabling sign-in on behalf of another user and does not provide environment management capabilities.
