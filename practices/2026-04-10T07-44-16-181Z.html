Provisioning a Power Pages site requires using the dedicated Power Pages design studio, which is accessed at https://make.powerpages.microsoft.com/. The Power Apps maker portal at make.powerapps.com is used for creating canvas and model-driven apps but not for provisioning Power Pages sites. The Power Automate portal at make.powerautomate.com is used for creating flows, and app.powerbi.com is the service for Power BI reports and dashboards.
