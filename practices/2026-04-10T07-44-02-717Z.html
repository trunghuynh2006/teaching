Power Pages enables low-code makers to build and deploy websites without needing to write code. Power Pages has tools to manage content, including the capability to expose data in Dataverse tables to external users such as customers. Power Pages cannot automate business processes or create documents, but it can be integrated with the rest of Microsoft Power Platform. Power Automate is used to automate business processes. AI Builder is used for extracting data from documents.
