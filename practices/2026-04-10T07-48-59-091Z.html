You are a business user who needs to guide other users through the stages and steps of a business process by using Microsoft Dataverse.

You need to create a business process flow.

What should you use?

Select only one answer.

The correct answer is Microsoft Power Automate because business process flows are created, configured, and published in Power Automate. These flows are tied to Dataverse tables and are designed to ensure users follow consistent steps for business processes such as sales, service, or onboarding. Power Apps is used for app development, not for creating business process flows. Power BI is used for analytics and reporting, not workflow design. Copilot Studio is used to build conversational agents, not structured business process flows.
