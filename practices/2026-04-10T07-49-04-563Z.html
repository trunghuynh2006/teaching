The item tests the candidate’s knowledge of the difference between canvas apps and model-driven apps and the use cases of the app types.

A canvas app connects to multiple data sources including SharePoint and Excel files stored in OneDrive for Business by using out-of-the-box connectors. Model-driven apps can use only Microsoft Dataverse as the underlying data store. Power Pages allow external customers and partners to access data in Microsoft Dataverse. Business process flows are used to standardize business processes, providing a streamlined UI for users to complete a process. Model-driven apps, Power Apps portals, and Business process flows cannot be used to read and write data from SharePoint and OneDrive.
