A Power Pages site requires a Dataverse database in the environment because site content, security, and integration with other Power Platform components are stored and managed in Dataverse. Without Dataverse, the site cannot be provisioned. Creating a DNS record or a Power Automate flow does not enable hosting, and an environment without Dataverse is insufficient to support a Power Pages site.
