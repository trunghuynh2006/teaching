## Content Quality Rules

- Titles must be clear, specific, and action-oriented (e.g., "Writing Professional Subject Lines", not "Email Subject Lines")
- Descriptions must summarize the learning outcome in 160 characters or fewer
- All text fields must use the specified target language throughout
- Tags must be lowercase, single-word or hyphenated (e.g., "email", "business-writing")
- Include 2–5 relevant tags per item
- Focus on practical, applicable skills that teachers can use directly in the classroom
- Never include culturally inappropriate or offensive content
